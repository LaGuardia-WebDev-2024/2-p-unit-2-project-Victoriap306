1. CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES

2 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
1 tsp of baking soda
1 tsp of salt
1 cup (2 sticks) of softened butter
3/4 cup of granulated sugar
3/4 cup of brown sugar
1 tsp of vanilla extract
2 large eggs

Instuctions

1. preheat oven to 375 F
2. Using a stand mixer or an electric hand mixer, cream together the salted butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar until combined.
3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing until thoroughly incorporated
4. Next, add the vanilla and mix well.
5. Add the flour, baking soda and salt, mixing until a soft dough forms.
6. Add in the chocolate chips, mix until evenly distributed.
7. Bake at 375Â° F for 7-9 minutes or until golden brown on the edges.
8. Allow the cookies to cool
9. Enjoy!
